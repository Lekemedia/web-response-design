About Us

Max Jonas Machinery.

Max Jonas Machinery engineering ltd is a machinery and resource engineering company, fabricating several brands of machines - ranging across agricultural/food processing machines, Waste Recycling, Mining, Material handling etc. and supplying several industrial materials like solid minerals, machine spare parts... Our organization consist of experienced engineers and technicians, passionate about meeting customers need, and possessing a good team spirit. We are the best in the machine design and fabrication industry in United State of America, with several success stories from our existing customers. You will be surprised at how great we can make from little-optimization of our locally sourced materials.
